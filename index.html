3D4EM

Design and implementation of an SDI for integrated environmental modelling in 3D

About

3D4EM is a research project funded by STW in which 14 organisations (universities, governement and companies) are working together to develop a countrywide and scalable 3D information infrastructure (SDI) for integrated 3D environmental modelling. This will enable easy use—and reuse—of 3D data to serve a wide variety of users and applications, and permit environmental software packages to better account for the third dimension.

Research lines

Reconstruction of 3D objects

Storage, update and dissemination 3D objects

3D environmental modelling

3D TOP10NL (Valkenburg)

Overview of the first available part of the 3D TOP10NL dataset, the dataset that will be used in the project.
